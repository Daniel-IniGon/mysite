About Me

My name is Daniel Iniesta González and I am currently making a career switch from web development into the video game industry. Started my IT career as a web developer, mainly bulding web apps in Drupal, Moodle and Wordpress.

From my childhood one of my fondest memories was playing video games or board games with my brothers. I loved to play all the time with them to any kind of game. When my older brother showed me the Warcraft 2 orc unit walking and told me that a programmer did it, I knew immediately that I would end up working creating video games.

I have been learning Video Game Development since my first Global Game Jam back in 2019. I am currently developing the game Orbitira's Tales (Moamoai indie studio first title).

Now it is time for a career switch. Let's make Video Games!
