About Me

My name is Daniel Iniesta González and I am currently making a career switch from web development into the video game industry. I have been learning in a self-taught way Video Game Development since my first Global Game Jam back in 2019. I Love to participate in this events to meet new people and work with them as a team. Always willing to help whoever could need my aid.

From my childhood one of my fondest memories was playing video games or board games with my brothers. When my older brother introduced me to Warcraft 2 and I saw orcs units walking it blew my mind, he then explained that it was the work of a programmer, and I knew immediately that I would end up working creating video games.

I use Unity3d and C# to develope games, but I have been using other languages and technologies in my profressional career as PHP, MySQL, CSS, etc. I am used to work with Git to keep the track of my projects. Also enjoy creating shaders using HLSL and the node based tool Shader Graph. I am currently collaborating in the development of the game Orbitria's Tales (Moamoai indie studio first title).

Now it is time for a career switch. Let's make Video Games!

Latest Projects

KanjiPractice - Demo of an app to practice Japanese Kanji

As a Japanese student I thought that building this little app, both to test my current skills and learn Japanese was a great idea.

You can practice the order stroke of the kanjis, also learn the pictograms main meaning and their 'On' and 'Kun' readings. There are 3 common words that contain that Kanji too.

WoWTFT - An autochess with the WoW pets

I think this is what some of Blizzard's fans expected when auto chess genre became so popular. So I created my own approach. This project does not meant to be a complete game, but a fun way to test the skills I have been learning so far.

Orbitria's Tales - MoaMoai

A mixed game between a turn based RPG and a roguelite.

Both your team and your enemies share the same energy to realize attacks and support abilities, so each turn it is a fight against each other and also for the resources.

Worked on many topics. Refactor of the abilities system. Built a tool to autogenerate a custom mesh on runtime to visualize the procedurally generated dungeon on a minimap.
